# Indaba Content Scheduling Brief
**Version:** Draft 1
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-24
**Status:** Active — African Proverbs cycle fully tested. All other content types WIP.

---

## 1. Overview

This document outlines how to manage, schedule, and publish all content across platforms (primarily WhatsApp Channel, VIP Group, and Website). The goal is consistent engagement, audience nurturing, and conversion to Patreon subscribers.

---

## 2. Content Types & Sources

### A. WhatsApp Channel (Primary Growth Engine)

| # | Content Type | Description | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Story Content (Love Back + future stories) | Broken into short parts with hook-driven endings | Author → Approved → Scheduled |
| 2 | African Proverbs Content ✅ | Image + proverb + meaning + engagement hook | Author Creates → Author Approves → Scheduled |
| 3 | Engagement Posts | Polls, quizzes, "React if…" prompts | Triggered every ~3–5 story posts OR when engagement dips |
| 4 | Conversion Posts | Soft CTA to Patreon or VIP group | Triggered after emotional or high-engagement story parts |

> ✅ = Fully tested end-to-end (Generate → Approve → Schedule → Send)

### B. VIP WhatsApp Group (Relationship Layer)

- Early-release chapters
- Voice notes (author insight, storytelling, personal tone)
- Occasional direct questions to group
- **Source: Author-driven only. No AI autoposting.**

### C. Website (Email Capture Layer)

- Delayed story chapters (after WA release)
- Email signup prompts
- **Source: Published after WhatsApp cycle completes**

---

## 3. Sending Schedule

### Daily WhatsApp Channel Schedule

| Time Window | Content Type |
|---|---|
| 07:30 – 09:00 | Proverb Post (engagement + relatability) |
| 12:30 – 14:00 | Story Part |
| 18:30 – 20:30 | Story Part OR Engagement Post |

### Posting Rules

- Max **2–3 posts per day**
- Minimum **3–4 hours spacing** between posts
- **No posting before 07:00**
- **No posting after 21:00**

---

## 4. Weekly Rhythm

- **5–6 days** active posting
- **1 lighter day** (reduced or no posting)
- **Every 10 days:** Quiz / Campaign to collect leads (phone numbers)

---

## 5. Do-Not-Send Rules

- No spam clustering (no back-to-back posts)
- No overly long text blocks without breaks
- No content without a hook or engagement CTA
- No late-night sends (after 21:00)
- No early-morning sends (before 07:00)

---

## 6. Content Flow (Pipeline)

1. **Creation** — Story: Author | Proverbs: Author / Claude Cowork
2. **Review & Approval** — Final approval by Author
3. **Queueing** — Assistant schedules content in order. Author approves what's in the send queue before it's sent.
4. **Publishing** — Based on daily schedule above
5. **Engagement Tracking** — Monitor reactions, replies, quiz responses

---

## 7. Conversion Flow

Channel → Engagement → Quiz → Direct Chat → VIP Group → Patreon

- Prioritize **consistency** over perfection
